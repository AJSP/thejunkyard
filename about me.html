About me

Hello, my name is Alec Judkins. I am 21 years old and I'm currently living in St. Paul, Minnesota. My hobbies are Gaming, going to the movies, drawing, writing stories, and acting. I'm looking to become a Game Designer and learn all the different skills required to be a game designer. I'm currently enrolled in the Web-Based Game Design program at St. Paul Community College and I'm hoping to get accepted into UW-Stout's Game Design program. In the meantime, I’d love to get into world building and character design. I am also looking to gain the experience to go into the workforce as a programmer or web designer as an alternate to game design. My dream is to move to California to work for Riot Games and design characters and help engineer their new fighting game.

The 3 words to describe me best are:

  • Patient: When I find something that requires creativity, whether it be a group project or a design I thought of one day on a passing whim, I love to put endless amounts of time into it until that vision comes to life.
  • Out-going: I’m very open to meeting new people and creating new experiences with anyone and everyone.
  • Inventive: I love to create new and original ideas and mold them into reality.
